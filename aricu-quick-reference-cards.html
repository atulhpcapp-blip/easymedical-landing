Quick Reference
For
Doctors
Clinical · Orders · AI

Everything you need to run your ICU round on ARICU — from your phone, in minutes.

First-Time Setup
  1. Open aricu.easymedicalsolutions.in in your phone browser
  2. Add to Home Screen (Share menu → Add to Home Screen) so it works like an app
  3. Log in with the credentials sent to your email
  4. Allow notifications → open Settings → Push Notifications → set your quiet hours & severity
Your Daily Round
  • Round Mode — see all your patients with live vitals, NEWS2, and alerts
  • Tap any patient → review vitals, scores, red flags, ABG/lab trends
  • Run AI Senior Consultant for complex cases — full analysis in ~60 sec
  • Place orders via Doctor Orders, or import directly from the AI's plan
  • Write your Daily Doctor Note; discharge via AI Discharge Summary
Key AI Tools
AI Senior Consultant 9 cr
Full 21-section case analysis with Indian-brand drugs & dosing.
AI Resident 3 cr
Background monitoring; flags drift before it becomes a red flag.
AI Discharge 5 cr
Auto-generated structured discharge summary.
ARICU PEARLS
Bedside wisdom that auto-surfaces on relevant patients. Submit your own.
🚨 Code Blue: One tap on a patient starts the timed resuscitation log and alerts the team. Use for cardiac arrest, witnessed pulse loss, or unresponsive + not breathing.

Quick Reference
For
Nurses
Vitals · Alerts · Codes

You're the eyes of the ICU. ARICU turns every vital you record into a live clinical picture.

First-Time Setup
  1. Open aricu.easymedicalsolutions.in in your phone browser
  2. Add to Home Screen so ARICU opens like an app
  3. Log in with the credentials sent to you
  4. Allow notifications to receive alerts for your patients
Your Shift Workflow
  • Record vitals each round — HR, BP, SpO₂, RR, Temp, GCS, urine output
  • ARICU auto-calculates NEWS2 & other scores from each entry
  • Watch for rising NEWS2 — escalate to the doctor early
  • Acknowledge alerts as you action them
  • Write nursing notes; record intake/output and observations
Key Features
Vitals Entry
Fast bedside entry; instantly updates all scores.
Alerts
Color-coded by severity — red = act now, yellow = watch.
Code Blue
One-tap timed resuscitation log with epinephrine clock.
Morning Brief
Overnight summary for clean shift handoff.
Tip: Accurate, timely vitals are what make ARICU's AI and scores reliable. When in doubt, record it — the system does the math.

Quick Reference
For
Admins
Users · NABH · Billing

Manage your hospital's ARICU — users, compliance, and billing — all from one dashboard.

First-Time Setup
  1. Open aricu.easymedicalsolutions.in on your computer (recommended for admin work)
  2. Log in with your hospital admin credentials
  3. Bookmark the admin dashboard for quick access
  4. Review your hospital settings — beds, wards, drug formulary
Your Responsibilities
  • Manage users — create, edit roles, deactivate departing staff
  • Monitor credits — track AI tool consumption against your plan
  • Review NABH Audit Log — every clinical action, time-stamped
  • Generate reports for compliance and leadership
  • Manage subscription — billing, tier, renewals
Key Tools
User Management
Create accounts, assign roles, control access.
NABH Audit Log
Immutable record of every action — who, what, when.
Credit Monitoring
Track AI usage; top up before running low.
Analytics
Hospital-level usage and scoring trends.
Important: Admins manage users & compliance but have no clinical authority — they can't place orders or alter patient records. This separation is part of NABH compliance.
