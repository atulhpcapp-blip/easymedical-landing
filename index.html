The story behind the software
"I was a doctor who couldn't tell you
what my hospital made last month."

Like most doctors who run their own clinic or hospital, Dr. Atul Rai was brilliant at medicine — but the business side was chaos. Referral commissions calculated on paper. Staff giving rough estimates. No idea which doctors sent the most patients or which area was growing. Month-end was guesswork.

He tried generic accounting software. Too complicated. Too generic. Not built for how hospitals actually work — with IP and OP patients, referral splits, package deals, visiting consultants, and credit collections.

So he built it himself. EasyMedical was built inside a real hospital, for real hospital problems. Every feature exists because Dr. Rai needed it for Om Hospital first.
