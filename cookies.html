Cookie Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2026 · Version: 1.0

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember you and how you use it.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

Cannot be disabled. Used for:

  • Authentication (Supabase session tokens)
  • Security (CSRF prevention)
  • Load balancing

2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Used for understanding product usage:

  • Mixpanel / Posthog (when implemented)
  • Anonymized usage patterns
  • No personal information

You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.

2.3 No Advertising Cookies

ARICU does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Your data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

  • Supabase (authentication)
  • Sentry (error monitoring — PHI masked)
  • Razorpay (payment processing on payment pages only)

Each has their own cookie policy.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security

Disabling essential cookies will break ARICU functionality.
