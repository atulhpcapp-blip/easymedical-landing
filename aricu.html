Common questions

Frequently asked.

How is ARICU different from existing hospital EMRs?
ARICU is purpose-built for ICU command center — not a general hospital EMR. It works alongside your existing EMR. You can keep using whatever you have for OPD, OT, billing, etc. ARICU specifically focuses on what makes ICU care safer: real-time monitoring, clinical scores, AI decision support, code blue, and discharge automation.
How does AI credit billing work?
Your subscription includes free credits each month. Use beyond that, and we bill ₹3/credit at month-end via Razorpay invoice. No upfront commitment. You can track usage in real-time from your dashboard, and we send SMS + email notifications when invoices are generated.
How accurate is the AI? What about hallucinations?
ARICU's AI is positioned as decision support — every suggestion is reviewed and accepted by the treating doctor. The AI works ONLY with data entered into ARICU (it never fabricates findings). Every recommendation is grounded in established guidelines (Surviving Sepsis, KDIGO, ISCCM, ICMR) shown openly in the Evidence Basis card. All AI interactions are logged for audit.
Where is patient data stored?
All data stored in India (AWS Mumbai region, Supabase Pro). End-to-end encryption at rest and in transit. DPDP Act compliant. NABH-ready audit trails. Your data never leaves Indian borders. Patient data is never used to train AI models.
Can we use ARICU for NABH accreditation?
Yes. ARICU includes NABH-grade audit logs (immutable, who-did-what-when), comprehensive clinical documentation, daily progress notes, round session tracking, code blue logging, and discharge summary compliance. You can export audit reports directly for NABH inspections.
What's the implementation time?
For pilot hospitals: 1-2 days. We create your hospital, you add beds and staff, your team starts using it. 1-hour onboarding call with Dr. Atul Rai personally walks your team through everything. No data migration needed since ARICU runs alongside existing systems.
Can doctors and nurses use ARICU on their phones?
Absolutely. ARICU is a Progressive Web App — install it on any phone (Android, iPhone, iPad, tablet). Push notifications wake the screen for Code Blue alerts. Real-time updates across all devices. Works on any Indian network including 4G.
Do you offer custom protocols for our hospital?
Yes — included in Professional and Enterprise plans, or free for Pilot Partners. Send us your hospital's specific ICU protocols (sepsis bundle, DKA, weaning, ventilator, etc.) and we'll integrate them into ARICU's protocol library, customized for your team.
What images can the AI analyze?
Doctors can upload chest X-rays, CT/MRI images, ECGs, lab report photos, wound photos, and other clinical images. The Senior Consultant Analysis (Pro+ plans) interprets them using GPT-4o vision and references findings in its recommendations. Each image attached costs +1 credit.
What if we want to cancel?
Pilot Partners: free 3-month pilot, no charges, leave anytime. Paid plans: cancel any time — no contracts, no termination fees. You retain access until end of billing period. Data export is available on request.
Is the AI HIPAA / DPDP compliant?
Yes — DPDP Act compliant. We use OpenAI's enterprise tier with zero data retention (queries are not stored or used to train models). Each AI query is processed in isolation. You can disable AI features entirely from the superadmin panel if your hospital has stricter requirements.
Can I see who used AI and how much it cost?
Yes. The /ai-usage page shows real-time credit consumption broken down by mode and by user. Hospital admins see total usage; superadmin sees per-doctor breakdown. Transparent billing — no surprises.
